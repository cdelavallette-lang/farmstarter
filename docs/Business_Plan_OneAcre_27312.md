# One-Acre Regenerative Farm Business Plan (27312)

## Snapshot
- Location: Pittsboro / Chatham County NC (27312)
- Land: 1 acre total, ~0.5 acre currently cleared
- Soil: heavy clay, rocky
- Objective: second income stream from specialty crops + season extension

## Enterprise Mix (initial)
- 0.20 ac: salad greens + herbs
- 0.10 ac: ginger/turmeric (premium specialty)
- 0.10 ac: cut flowers
- 0.10 ac: pollinator/perennial support strips

## Regenerative Soil Strategy
- Permanent bed system
- Cover crop cycles (warm and cool season)
- Compost-first fertility strategy
- Reduced tillage and compaction control

## Pest/Wildlife Strategy
- Exclusion: netting/row cover/fencing where required
- Biological: habitat strips for beneficial insects
- Monitoring: weekly scouting + sticky traps + camera checks

## Automation Strategy
- Phase 1: soil moisture sensors + smart irrigation control
- Phase 2: drone scouting + greenhouse climate controls

## Revenue Model (conservative)
- Year 1 gross target: ~$28,000
- Year 2 gross target: ~$48,500

## Priority Funding Path
1. NRCS EQIP
2. NC AgVentures
3. NRCS CSP
4. USDA REAP
5. USDA VAPG (if value-added line is ready)
6. SARE (if structured as on-farm trial)
