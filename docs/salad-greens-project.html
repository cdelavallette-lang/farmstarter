Project Overview

Space 0.20 acre (8,700 sq ft)
Timeline March 2026 - Dec 2027
Revenue Target $12,000 - $15,000/year
Crops Lettuce, Greens, Herbs

High-demand, fast-growing crops for weekly sales. Focus on succession planting for continuous harvest March through November.

📋 Phase 1: Planning & Grants (January - March 2026)

Week 1-2: Grant Research (January 1-14)

⏱️ Time: 6-8 hours
  • Review NRCS EQIP drip irrigation requirements
  • Draft irrigation system design & get quotes (3 suppliers)
  • Contact local NRCS office for conservation visit
💰 Estimated Costs: $0 (planning phase)

Week 3-4: NC AgVentures Prep (January 15-31)

⏱️ Time: 8-10 hours
  • Complete application form
  • Gather documentation (site plan, budget, narrative)
  • Submit by April 30 deadline
🎯 Deadline: April 30, 2026

February: Conservation Planning

⏱️ Time: 12-16 hours
  • NRCS conservation planner site visit
  • Finalize EQIP application
  • Soil test entire 0.20 acre area
  • Order compost (3-5 cubic yards)
💰 Estimated Costs: $50-$100 soil test + $150-$250 compost

March 1-15: Submit Applications

⏱️ Time: 8 hours
  • Submit EQIP application (March 1-15 deadline)
  • Mark out bed layout on field
  • Spread compost if soil workable
  • Order early season seeds
💰 Seeds & Supplies: $50-$100

🔧 Phase 2: Infrastructure Installation (April - May 2026)

April: Install Irrigation System

⏱️ Time: 16-24 hours
  • Submit NC AgVentures application (by April 30)
  • Order irrigation supplies if EQIP approved
  • Install mainline, drip tape, timers, filters (12-16 hrs with helper)
  • Test system and adjust for even coverage
💰 Irrigation System: $800-$1,200 (EQIP covers $400-$900)

May: Planting Begins

⏱️ Time: 20-24 hours
  • Plant first succession of lettuce, arugula, spinach
  • Transplant basil and herbs (200-300 plants)
  • Install row covers over greens beds
  • Plant second succession
  • Ongoing: Irrigate 2-3x per week
🎯 Milestone: First harvest expected June 2026

🌱 Phase 3: Harvest & Sales (June - November 2026)

Month Main Tasks Hours/Week Revenue Projection
June First harvests, begin weekly sales, plant summer succession 12-16 $800-$1,200
July-Aug Peak production, bi-weekly planting, buyer outreach 16-20 $2,000-$2,500/mo
Sept-Nov Continue harvests, plant fall greens, prep for winter 14-18 $1,500-$2,000/mo

👥 Buyer Outreach Actions

  • Contact 3-5 local restaurants with weekly availability
  • Set up farmers market booth or CSA partnership
  • Promote farm stand sales
  • Use email templates from Buyer_Outreach_Templates.md

✨ Key Success Factors

  1. Succession Planting: Plant new crops every 2-3 weeks for continuous harvest
  2. Pest Management: Install row covers early to prevent flea beetles and aphids
  3. Irrigation Discipline: Check soil moisture 3x/week, adjust timer seasonally
  4. Buyer Relationships: Establish 3-5 reliable weekly customers by July 2026
  5. Record Keeping: Use Farm_Forms_Pack templates for tracking plantings and sales
