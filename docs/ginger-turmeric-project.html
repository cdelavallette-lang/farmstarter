Project Overview

Space 0.10 acre (4,350 sq ft)
Timeline March 2026 - Nov 2027
Revenue Target $8,000 - $12,000/year
Growing Cycle 7 months in tunnel

High-value specialty crops that sell for $8-$15/lb. Requires protected culture (high tunnel) and 7-month growing season. Year 1 focuses on infrastructure, Year 2 on harvest and sales.

📋 Phase 1: Grants & Infrastructure Planning (January - August 2026)

January-February: Grant Research

⏱️ Time: 12-15 hours
  • Research NRCS EQIP high tunnel cost-share (50-75% reimbursement)
  • Get quotes from 3 tunnel suppliers (30x48 size)
  • Work with NRCS conservation planner on application
  • Complete NC AgVentures application including tunnel costs
💰 High Tunnel Cost: $5,000-$8,000
EQIP covers: $2,500-$6,000
NC AgVentures covers: Up to $10,000

March 2026: Submit Applications

⏱️ Time: 8 hours
  • Submit EQIP application (March 15 deadline)
  • Mark out tunnel location (flat, well-drained)
  • Soil test tunnel site
  • Critical: Order ginger and turmeric rhizomes for May delivery
⚠️ CRITICAL DEADLINE: Order seed rhizomes by late March. They're shipped May for spring planting.

April-August: Waiting & Planning

⏱️ Time: Variable
  • Expect EQIP decision (4-8 weeks after deadline)
  • Submit NC AgVentures application as backup
  • If EQIP approved by June: proceed to Phase 2
  • If EQIP delayed: consider postponing to 2027
🎯 Timeline Decision Point: EQIP approval = September installation → May 2027 planting

🔧 Phase 2: High Tunnel Installation (August-September 2026)

August: Order & Site Prep

⏱️ Time: 16-20 hours
  • Once EQIP approved, order high tunnel (8-12 week lead time)
  • Clear and level tunnel site (may need small equipment rental)
  • Prepare raised beds or mounded rows
💰 Site Prep: $200-$400 equipment rental + $600-$1,000 materials

September: Tunnel Installation

⏱️ Time: 24-32 hours (2-3 days with help)
  • High tunnel delivery and installation
  • Install drip irrigation inside tunnel
  • Test ventilation, doors, and climate controls
  • Recommendation: Hire professional installer ($1,000-$1,500)
✅ Completed by September 30, 2026
Ready for May 2027 planting season

🌱 Phase 3: Planting & Growing (May - October 2027)

May 2027: Pre-Sprouting & Planting

⏱️ Time: 12-16 hours
  • Pre-sprout rhizomes indoors in moist potting mix (10-14 days)
  • Maintain temperature 75-80°F
  • Prepare tunnel beds with compost (2-3" deep)
  • Transplant sprouted rhizomes 6-8" apart
💰 Planting Supplies:
Seed rhizomes: $500-$800
Compost: $300-$500

June-October 2027: Growing Season

⏱️ Time: 4-6 hours/week
  • Irrigation: Check soil moisture 3x/week, water deeply (2-3 hrs/week)
  • Weeding: Hand-weed around plants (1-2 hrs/week)
  • Monitoring: Check for pests, monitor leaf health (30 min/week)
  • Fertilizing: Monthly compost tea applications (1 hr/month)
  • Tunnel Management: Maintain 75-85°F day, 65-75°F night
🎯 Leaf emergence: 4-6 weeks
Full canopy: August
Ready to harvest: October-November

🔪 Phase 4: Harvest & Curing (November-December 2027)

November 2027: Dig & Harvest

⏱️ Time: 16-24 hours
  • Stop watering, allow plants to die back (signals maturity)
  • Dig plants carefully with fork or hands
  • Wash rhizomes gently, remove excess soil
📊 Expected Yield:
Ginger: 600-900 lbs
Turmeric: 200-400 lbs
Total: 800-1,300 lbs fresh

December 2027: Curing & Sales Prep

⏱️ Time: 8-12 hours
  • Cure rhizomes in warm (75-80°F), humid area for 7-10 days
  • Sort by size and quality (A-grade fresh, B-grade processing)
  • Save best rhizomes for replanting next year
  • Package and prepare delivery to buyers

🛍️ Phase 5: Sales & Market Development

Target Markets & Pricing

  • Specialty Grocery Stores: Whole Foods, co-ops - $10-$15/lb wholesale
  • Restaurants: High-end, juice bars - $12-$16/lb
  • Farmers Markets: Direct consumer - $12-$18/lb retail
  • CSA Add-Ons: Existing customers - $10-$15/lb

Marketing Timeline (October 2027)

  • Contact 5-10 specialty grocers and restaurants with availability notice
  • Use Buyer_Outreach_Templates.md for professional emails
  • Post on social media about upcoming harvest
  • Deliver product samples to interested buyers
  • Negotiate pricing and delivery schedules

✨ Critical Success Factors

  1. Tunnel Timing: Must be installed by September 2026 for May 2027 planting
  2. Quality Seed Rhizomes: Source from reputable suppliers (Hawaiian Ginger Co., Tropilab, local growers)
  3. Temperature Control: Maintain consistent 75-85°F day/65-75°F night in tunnel
  4. Irrigation Precision: Drip irrigation critical for even moisture
  5. Market Development: Start buyer outreach 6 months before harvest (April 2027)
