# Google Sites Deployment Kit (Free)

## Goal
Publish a grant-ready showcase site with free Google tools.

## Free Stack
- Google Sites
- Google Drive
- Google Docs
- Google Sheets
- Google Slides
- Google Forms

## Suggested Pages
1. Home
2. Business Plan
3. Financials & Projections
4. Grant Readiness
5. Regenerative + Automation Strategy
6. Contact / Partnership

## Build Steps (60-90 min)
1. Create Drive folder: FarmGrowth_Grant_Site
2. Upload files from this workspace
3. Open CSV files in Google Sheets
4. Create charts:
   - Monthly Gross vs Operating Expenses
   - Net Cashflow by Month
5. Build pages in Google Sites
6. Embed charts, KPI table, and contact form
7. Publish as link-shareable

- Program alignment (EQIP, CSP, AgVentures, REAP)
- Extension collaboration note
- Quarterly KPI reporting commitment
