# Regenerative One-Acre Farm Grant Pitch (27312)

## Mission
Build a profitable, regenerative one-acre specialty crop farm using soil-first practices, season extension, and practical automation.

## Starting Point
- Chatham County, NC (27312)
- ~0.5 acre active today, 1 acre total
- Heavy clay/rocky soil constraints

## Funding Use
1. Soil-building and drainage improvements
2. High tunnel/greenhouse infrastructure
3. Drip irrigation and controls
4. Pest and wildlife exclusion systems
5. Monitoring automation (sensors + drone scouting)

## 24-Month Outcomes
- Year-round production under protected culture
- Gross potential from ~$28k (Year 1) to ~$48.5k (Year 2)
- Measured improvements in soil, water use, and crop reliability

## Success Metrics
- Yield and revenue per bed
- Buyer retention and channel diversity
- Soil and water performance indicators
- Reduced crop losses from pest/wildlife/weather
